STICKY NAVBAR - FIXED VERSION
==============================

✅ SOLUTION: Changed from STICKY to FIXED positioning

WHAT WAS CHANGED:
-----------------

1. TOP BAR (.top-bar)
   - position: fixed (instead of sticky)
   - top: 0
   - left: 0, right: 0, width: 100%
   - z-index: 999 (on top)
   
2. HEADER (header)
   - position: fixed (instead of sticky)
   - top: 56px (below the top bar)
   - left: 0, right: 0, width: 100%
   - z-index: 998 (below top bar)

3. BODY (body)
   - padding-top: 136px (prevents content from hiding under fixed nav)
   - Top bar: ~56px
   - Header: ~80px

WHY THIS WORKS:
---------------
- Fixed positioning keeps navbar visible at all times
- No jumping because both elements are fixed together
- No layout shift because body padding compensates
- Smooth scrolling of content beneath navbar
